About This Project

This interactive mapping application was created to visualize the relationship between community health challenges and available resources in Seattle. By combining data on homeless health statistics with emergency food service locations, we aim to provide insights that can help inform community support initiatives and resource allocation.

Our goal is to make complex geographic and demographic data accessible and understandable, helping communities better understand and address health and social service needs.
